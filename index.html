@ConcernedApe

Eric Barone, also known by his alias ConcernedApe, is an American video game developer, video game designer, artist, composer, and musician. He is best known for independently creating the video game Stardew Valley. In 2021, he announced that he was working on a game called Haunted Chocolatier. As of 2023, it has no set release date.

Stardew Valley

Barone began working on Stardew Valley in 2012 and released it in 2016. He was praised for creating the game independently, as its sole designer, programmer, animator, artist, composer, and writer. To complete the game, Barone worked 10 hours a day, seven days a week, for four and a half years; living in Capitol Hill, Seattle. For part of that time, he lived with his parents to save money after which he and his then girlfriend moved in together.
Barone initially released Stardew on the PC before later developing it for other consoles. By March 2022, he had sold over 20 million copies.
In 2017, he was named by Forbes magazine in their list "30 Under 30: Games" due to his work on Stardew Valley. Since 2019, Barone has been assisted on Stardew Valley by another designer.
